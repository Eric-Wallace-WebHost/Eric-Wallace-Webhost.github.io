Hello

I'm Eric Wallace, an undergraduate at the University of Maryland studying Computer Engineering. I am currently conducting research in GPU Computing and Computational Aerodynamics at the Alfred Gessow Rotorcraft Center. My current publications are available here .

I also work on an awesome startup venture named Pinpointer that helps to put the 4 billion people in the world without an address on the map.

I am planning to pursue graduate studies in Computer Science in the areas of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, especially in the areas of Reinforcement Learning for game agents.

If you are interested in discussing my research, venture, or anything of interest, please reach out using the links at the bottom of the front page.
