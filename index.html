Hello

I'm Eric Wallace, an undergraduate at the University of Maryland studying Computer Engineering. I am currently conducting research in GPU Computing and Computational Aerodynamics at the Alfred Gessow Rotorcraft Center. My current publications are available here .

I have broad interests in the areas of Machine Learning using Deep Networks, where I currently focus on Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning.
